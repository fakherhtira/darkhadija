About

Our museum gives you the Opportunity to join us and learn cultural stuffs. In this Week you'll be able to learn the basics of Weaving. It is also a good Opportunity to come and take a look at our museum and its story. We're waiting for you to join us. Don't come by yourself. Bring your will to learn and your patient and your desire with you. Dar Khadija welcomes you.

Awards

This Opportunity isn't just to learn and meet other people or to get to know our story and our museum. You won't leave empty handed. Each one of you will get a prize for his work. We're doing this to motivate you to come and join us. Who is the one that would take the biggest prize here? It's you! come and win with us a good experience and a big prize. This is the chance.
