Program

In this online experience, The potter will start by sharing his pottery practice. Then you will learn the basics of the pottery for 30 minutes and you will have 20 minutes to go deep into your practice next he'll go around and correct your creations for 20 minutes. Afterwards, you’ll share your questions and reflections on the practice. You need to download Zoom onto your phone or computer before the experience starts.

How to practice?

To get into the experience you have to:

Use Zoom to participate on your desktop or mobile device. After you book, you’ll receive an email with a link and details on how to join.
