POST-COVID

REOPENING

The museum reopened on February,21st to welcome you under the best possible conditions. We're looking for your safety. That's why all visitors, including those entitled to free admission, must book a time slot. Bookings can be done through the online ticketing service.

HOURS & ADMISSION

Opening hours

The museum is open daily except Mondays. Last entry 1 hour before closure. Visitors will be asked to prepare themselves to leave 30 minutes before closure.

Visits

Visit as a family

Enjoy Dar Khadija experience with your children at no extra cost – admission to our events. It is free for all visitors under the age of 10. The museum also offers guided tours, practical workshops and storytelling sessions in the palace, as well as a audio-guide and selection of online media resources.

VISIT AS A GROUP

The museum offers a range of activities and tools for visiting the museum with groups of school children, disabled visitors and more: how to organise your visit, training, Dar Khadija membership, etc.
